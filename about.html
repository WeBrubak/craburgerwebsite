History of Craburger In 1993 in the city of Baltimore the Krabs family came together with a dream to create a brand in the fast food experience that no one had done before. They decided to harness the strength of the Baltimore Harbor and create Craburger. A seafood fast-food brand with the goal of bringing the delicious sea to people all over the east coast.

The Founder Eugene Krabs and his daughter Pearl decided to pool their funds together and open the first location in the inner Harbor close to the Aquarium. Additionally, the company opened with multiple trucks that allow for travel anywhere in the upper east coast area.

The Krabs family has the intention of expanding their brand all over the country and letting all of the United States try the succulent taste of seafood with crabs, fish, shrimp and other incredible foods all across this nation.
